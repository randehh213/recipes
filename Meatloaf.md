Classic Meatloaf
Ingredients

    2 lbs. lean ground beef
    1 cup Italian breadcrumbs
    2 eggs
    1 cup onion, diced
    2 tsp garlic powder
    2 tsp chopped parsley
    1 tsp salt
    1 tsp pepper
    1/2 cup bbq sauce

For the Topping:

    1/3 cup ketchup
    2 tbsp brown sugar
    1 tsp yellow mustard
